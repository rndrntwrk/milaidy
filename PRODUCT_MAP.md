# Product Map

## Core surfaces

- `CLI`
  install, setup, config, start, dashboard, doctor, model/provider management
- `Dashboard`
  chat, apps, pairing, stream, settings
- `Runtime`
  agents, lifecycle, memory/state, actions, triggers, plugins, skills
- `Knowledge`
  runtime-native corpus in `knowledge/`, documentation in `docs/`, structured
  operator material in `docs/ops/`
- `Connectors`
  Discord, Telegram, Twitter, Slack, WhatsApp, and future channels
- `Deployment`
  local-first default, managed and production deployment paths documented
  separately

## Alice relationship

Alice is a Milady runtime/operator deployment with:

- stricter identity and trust requirements
- stronger deploy and runtime validation requirements
- cross-repo knowledge synchronization with `555-bot`

Milady remains the host/runtime product. Alice remains one high-trust operating
profile built on top of it.
