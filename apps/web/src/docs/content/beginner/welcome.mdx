# What is Milady?

Milady is a personal AI assistant that runs on your own computer. You download it, pick a character, give it access to a language model, and talk to it. It keeps its memory on your machine, not in someone else's cloud. It can also reach out into Discord, Telegram, iMessage, and other places — so the same assistant you chat with on your laptop can also answer messages on your behalf somewhere else.

That's the short version. Here's the longer one.

## What Milady actually does

- **Talks to you.** Text and voice. Desktop app, web dashboard, or the companion overlay that sits beside your work.
- **Remembers you.** Conversations, documents you upload, facts you tell it. All stored locally by default.
- **Connects to places.** Discord servers, Telegram chats, iMessage threads, and a growing list of other platforms. You pick which ones.
- **Has a personality.** Each character (think of it like a profile) has its own voice, avatar, and style. You can pick one of the built-ins or customize your own.
- **Uses whatever brain you give it.** Local models via Ollama, or cloud APIs like OpenAI, Anthropic, OpenRouter, Eliza Cloud — you decide.

<Callout kind="tip">
Milady is **local-first**. That means your data lives on your machine unless you opt in to send it somewhere. If you use a cloud provider like OpenAI for the language model, your messages get sent to them for inference — but the conversation history, character files, and anything you upload stay with you.
</Callout>

## What Milady is not

- **Not a chatbot product you log into.** There's no account at milady.ai. You download the app and it runs on your computer.
- **Not a voice-only toy.** The voice and avatar are great, but Milady is really a full agent that can do work — respond to messages, run scheduled tasks, watch for events.
- **Not married to one model provider.** If OpenAI raises their prices tomorrow or Ollama ships a new model, you can switch in settings. No lock-in.
- **Not a replacement for a human assistant.** It does not have your login credentials for anything you haven't given it. It does not read your email unless you connect it. It does not make phone calls. Every capability is opt-in.

## Is this for you?

You'll probably like Milady if:

- You want an AI assistant but you don't want to hand over every conversation to a cloud service.
- You're already running local models (or want to start) and need a friendly interface.
- You want one assistant that follows you across Discord, Telegram, and your desktop.
- You enjoy making things feel more yours — custom character, custom voice, custom avatar.

You'll probably not like Milady if:

- You want a turnkey hosted chatbot with zero setup. That's a different product.
- You don't want to pick a language model provider and you refuse to run anything locally.
- You want something that auto-reads your calendar, inbox, and files without you explicitly connecting them.
